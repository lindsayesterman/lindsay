Mercury

Mercury allows users to input a special date and design digital postcards for anyone. Users can type in the day of their anniversary, birthday, or holiday and a gorgeous NASA astronomy picture taken that day will appear. Then one can personalize the picture with an appreciative message.

Roughly a quarter million postcards are sent annually. While physical mailing is environmentally detrimental, Mercury provides a great alternative for something more personal. Check out a few Nasa apod dates yourself. Stack: HTML, CSS, JavaScript, jQuery, Nasa API.
