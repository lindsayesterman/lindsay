Screentime Saver

Screentime Saver is an app dedicated to aspiring digital minimalists. Users can post screen times, make a profile, and compete with friends to win the lowest times of the week! Digital minimalism is about working with tech in a healthy way. Screentime Saver helps users to maximize tech's benefits and minimize its drawbacks. Stack: React, Node, Express, PostgreSQL, CSS.
